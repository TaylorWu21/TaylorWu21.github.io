Projects

Stalk Market

A website for local Utah farmer markets. Vendors from markets can create accounts for their businesses and customers can view their profiles to find out more about the vendors and what markets they are a part of.

Check Out Stalk Market!
The Haps

An app that grabs events from City Weekly API and places them on our custom Google Maps that provides addition information on the app. Users can log in and create events or comment on them.

Check Out The Haps!
Book Keeper

Website that uses Google Books API to help users create a digital library to keep track of their books. Users can view other people's libraries. If they like them enough they can follow them and comment on their books.

Check Out Book Keeper!
